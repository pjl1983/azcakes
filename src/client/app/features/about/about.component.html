Meet your baker

Elizabeth Bradley’s unlikely journey began with an inspirational nudge from her mother who told her “she could do anything in her life she wanted too.” Armed with those encouraging words and a giant new mixer, gifted to her by her ever supportive father, the next day she baked her first cake for a family friend. Thousands of delicious cakes and 19 years later, she’s happiest when creating one of her distinctive pieces. “For my new and repeat clients alike, I eagerly await your special occasion that will call for an amazing edible, art sculpture from AZ CAKES.”

19 YEARS plus and counting.
THANKS MOM AND DAD!
