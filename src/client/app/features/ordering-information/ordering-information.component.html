Pricing & Selection

All of our cakes are custom designed. A phone consultation, E-mail or in person consultation is required to determine a price. Any cake displayed can be custom made to accommodate any number of guests as well as any colors desired.

We are a private, custom cake studio located in North Scottsdale, near the Boulders Resort in Carefree, AZ. We are a licensed facility with full approval from the city of Phoenix, & The Maricopa County Health Department.

All cake orders must be ordered at least 7 – 10 days in advance to assure availability.

AZ Cakes Does Not Ship Cakes, so sorry!


Minimum Order

There is a $100 minimum order on all cakes.


Deposits

A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a party cake The balance is due one week prior to date.
A $100 non-refundable deposit is required to reserve a wedding cake The balance is due two weeks prior to date.


Date Change

In the event that your celebration date is changed or cancelled, your deposit will remain valid for one year after the original deposit date and can be used towards another cake order. We require one week notice on party cakes and one month notice on wedding cakes. Without proper notification, Your payments will be forfeited.


Delivery

A delivery charge of $25 – $75 is added based on mileage.

No deliveries on Sunday!
